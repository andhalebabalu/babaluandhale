About NPTEL SWAYAM

NPTEL (National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning) is a joint initiative of IITs and IISc Bangalore, funded by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. Courses are taught by professors from India's premier technical institutions and are examined with proctored assessments. SWAYAM is the Government of India's national online education platform. Elite status is awarded to candidates scoring 60%+.
